About

My interest for code started in late 2022, but my interest for problem solving have been with me since childhood. Asking questions, wanting to know how things work, working hard to understand and succeeding, are some ways to describe who I am as a person, and now developer. My greater vision regarding my projects, is to create solutions that are comprehensible to my collaborators, whilst making the life of my users easier and more enjoyable.

I have an 8 year background in highly specialized acute healthcare, where I played a key role in descicion making, problem solving and group leadership. This led to me learning how to work efficiently under pressure, how to collaborate with people from different walks of life, and how to teach, which in my opinion is fundamental to be a good developer and team member.

Currently I am studying at Chas Academy, spending most of my days learning C# and .NET, solving labs on my own or with my peers. When I am not coding, I spend my time reading high fantasy or sci-fi, consuming 90s media, or moving around outside, if the Swedish weather allows it (which is always; those who know, know).
